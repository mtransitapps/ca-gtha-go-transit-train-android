Full description:
This app adds Greater Toronto & Hamilton Area GO trains information to MonTransit.

This app provides the planned schedule as well as real time service statuses and news from YouTube and @GOtransit, @GOtransitBR, @GOtransitKT, @GOtransitLE, @GOtransitLW, @GOtransitMI, @GOtransitRH and @GOtransitST on Twitter.

GO Transit trains serve Toronto, Hamilton, Halton, Peel, York, Durham, Niagara, Waterloo, Peterborough, Simcoe, Dufferin, Wellington in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display trains information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by the GO Transit.
http://www.gotransit.com/publicroot/en/schedules/DeveloperResources.aspx

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-gtha-go-transit-train-android

This app is not related with the GO Transit and Metrolinx.

Permissions:
- Other: required for real-time service statuses and to read news from YouTube and Twitter
